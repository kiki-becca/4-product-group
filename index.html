Benefits of Having a Wireless Charger

Faster than Ever

“Fast-charging” option available, unlike many store-bought charging cords. (10W charging speed)

Minimalistic

The charger is wooden so it blends into furniture and doesn’t look like a mess or a disruption. We have many different types of wood to blend into or accent any furniture you may have. They are also incredible lightweight, at only about 0.14lbs, or 64 grams.

Affordable

As cheap as $39, depending on what wood you choose. Many other chargers on the market can get up to around $80.

Compatible

Compatible with iPhone XS, XS Max, XR, 8 and 8 Plus, Samsung Galaxy S10/S10+, Google Pixel 3 and 3 XL and all Qi enabled devices. Compatible with all KerfCase wood phone cases, and works with most other cases up to 3 mm thick.

We know design.

Our products use minimalistic, lightweight, wood-based design.

Our Priorities

The real wood we use is sourced locally and sustainably, and whenever possible reclaimed from damaged local trees. Every piece of wood has a story, whether it's a piece of reclaimed flooring, or a beautiful burled wood from a local tree which was damaged in a storm. The craftsman's woodworking is a continuation of this story, connecting you to the history of this natural material.
